About Our Screen Printing

Screen printing is a traditional printing method that produces vibrant, durable designs. It's ideal for larger quantities and designs with solid colors. We use high-quality plastisol inks that maintain their brightness through repeated washing.

Screen Printing FAQs

What is the minimum order quantity for screen printing?

Our minimum order for screen printing is typically 24 pieces. For smaller quantities, we recommend our DTG or DTF printing services.

How many colors can I include in my screen print design?

We can print up to 8 colors for most orders. Each color requires a separate screen, so pricing increases with the number of colors in your design.

What file formats do you accept for screen printing?

Vector files (.ai, .eps, .pdf) work best for screen printing. We can also work with high-resolution PNG files with transparent backgrounds.

What is the maximum print size for screen printing?

Our standard maximum print area is 14" x 16" for adult garments. Youth sizes and specialty items may have smaller maximum print areas.

Do you offer specialty inks or printing techniques?

Yes, we offer specialty options including metallic inks, glow-in-the-dark, puff prints, and water-based inks. These options may incur additional charges.
