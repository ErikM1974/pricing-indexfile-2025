Art Requirements & Fees

Artwork Requirements

  • Vector Files Preferred: .AI, .EPS, .PDF (with fonts outlined)
  • High-Resolution Raster: .PNG, .JPG (300 DPI minimum)
  • Design Files: .PSD with layers preserved
  • Embroidery: .DST files or vector art for digitizing
  • Screen Printing: Separated artwork with Pantone colors specified

How to Enter in ShopWorks

  1. Open Order Entry: Navigate to the customer's order
  2. Add Line Item: Click "Add Item" or press F7
  3. Enter Part Number: Type the part number (e.g., "GRT-50")
  4. Quantity: Enter 1 for flat fees, or hours for hourly work
  5. Description: Auto-populates from part number
  6. Save: Press Enter or click Save to add to order
Pro Tip: For custom design work exceeding 2 hours, use multiple GRT-75 line items with quantity = hours worked.

Important Notes:

  • Art fees are waived for repeat orders using existing artwork
  • Complex designs requiring more than 2 hours will be quoted
  • Rush service requires manager approval
  • All artwork becomes property of Northwest Custom Apparel
