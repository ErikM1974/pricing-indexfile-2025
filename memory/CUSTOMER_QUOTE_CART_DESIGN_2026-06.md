## DTG parity rules

**Summary:** DTG staff pricing is fully canonicalized in two algorithm-identical implementations: the browser service shared_components/js/dtg-pricing-service.js (used by the staff inline-form builder, the calculator, AND the customer product page's existing tabs) and the server module caspio-pricing-proxy/lib/dtg-canonical-pricing.js exposed as POST /api/dtg/quote-pricing (what the staff builder actually submits through). Formula: per-style garment cost (min valid size price) / tier.MarginDenominator + SUM of per-location print costs (combos like LC_FB split on '_' and sum, garment counted once) â†’ ONE Math.ceil(x*2)/2 half-dollar-ceil â†’ + integer size upcharges. Tier comes from the quantity POOLED across every DTG line item (all styles/colors/sizes) â€” live Caspio tiers: 1-23 (MD 0.55, LTM_Fee $50), 24-47/48-71/72+ (MD 0.53). LTM (<24 pcs) amortizes as Math.floor((LTM_Fee/pooledQty)*100)/100 added to every unit â€” the honest effective per-unit price the customer page needs. Verified quirks with live data: the 1-23 BASE price is cheaper than 24-47 (margin-denominator inversion), LTM orders use 24-47 print costs because the '12-23' cost rows never match the '1-23' label, and two UI-offerable combos (FF_JB, JF_FB) are unpriceable. Worked examples below were produced by executing the canonical module against live PC61/PC54 bundles. Recommended cart design: call POST /api/dtg/quote-pricing with one locationCode + all pooled DTG lines, exactly as the staff builder does, restricted to the 9 supported codes.

### LTM rules

## DTF parity rules

**Summary:** DTF pricing is fully API-driven via GET /api/pricing-bundle?method=DTF (tiersR/allDtfCostsR/freightR/rulesR) with garment cost from the BLANK bundle.
