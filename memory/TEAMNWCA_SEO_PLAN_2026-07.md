*2026-07-14. Companion to `reference/net-seo-recovery-roadmap-2026-07.html` (Mehar's .net plan)
and `memory/blog-content-differentiation.md` (Lane Split v2). The two plans are designed to

## The one-paragraph strategy

.net rebuilds **local + service** visibility ("custom embroidery near me", city pages, GBP).
teamnwca owns **product + style + tools** visibility (style numbers, brand catalogs,
comparisons, quote builders). Every .net page funnels product/pricing intent to teamnwca;
every teamnwca post cross-links .net for education and (new) local-service intent. Neither
site ever writes the other's query class. Success = combined non-brand commercial traffic
and quote requests, measured monthly, October 2026 checkpoint.

  /dtg-calculator/ for retirement).
