How It Works

1 Maintenance Fee Calculation

The 1% maintenance commission is based on meeting your quarterly revenue baseline from ALL assigned accounts combined.

  • If baseline is met: Earn 1% of total quarterly revenue from your accounts
  • If baseline is NOT met: $0 commission for that quarter

Baseline is calculated from the trailing 12-month average when accounts were assigned.

2 New Store Commissions & Setup Bonuses

New online stores earn higher commission rates during the initial growth period, plus a one-time setup bonus to reward the rep for the time and effort to establish the store.
