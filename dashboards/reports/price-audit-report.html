What to Change — Training Notes

Customer-Supplied Garment Embroidery (DECG)
Our 2026 DECG price is $20–$28/pc depending on qty tier. Many orders were charged $15–$20. Action: When a customer brings their own garments, use the quote builder — it calculates the correct tier automatically.
Additional Logo Locations (AL)
AL pricing gap: $696.00. 2026 garment AL is $7–$10/pc by tier, caps are $5.25–$6.50. Old pricing was $5–$8, sometimes lower. Action: Always add AL as a separate line — the builder calculates the tier price.
Less Than Minimum (LTM) Fee — 16 Orders Missing
Orders with 7 or fewer pieces should include a $50 LTM fee. That's $800.00 in missed revenue. Action: The quote builder adds LTM automatically — baked into per-piece price. Just double-check orders under 8 pcs.
Digitizing (DD)
Standard digitizing is $100 in 2026, revisions (DDE/DDT/DGT) are $50. Some orders charged $50 for a full DD. Action: New designs = DD ($100). Revisions to existing = DDE/DDT ($50). The Service Price Cheat Sheet has all codes.
Patch Setup Fee (GRT-50) Missing
2 orders were missing the $50 patch setup fee. Action: Every laser patch or embroidered patch order needs a GRT-50 setup fee. The builder adds it automatically.
