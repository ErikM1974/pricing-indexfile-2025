Why Fabric Content Matters for DTG

Direct-to-Garment printing is a precise digital printing method that requires specific fabric types to achieve the best results. Unlike screen printing or embroidery, DTG ink bonds best with natural cotton fibers.

  • Best Results: 100% cotton garments provide the most vibrant colors and longest-lasting prints
  • Good Results: 50% or higher cotton blends work well for most designs
  • Poor Results: Polyester, performance fabrics, and low-cotton blends don't accept DTG ink properly
