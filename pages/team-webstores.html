Team webstore FAQ

What is a team apparel store?

It's an online store we build with your logo, so players, parents, and fans order their own gear on demand. We embroider it and ship it, so you never stock inventory or collect order forms.

How is pricing calculated?

Each item is priced at our first-tier (1–7 piece) left-chest embroidery rate plus a flat $6 handling fee — everyone pays the same fair per-piece rate, with no group minimums and no inventory to buy up front. There's a one-time $300 setup fee to build and brand your store. People order what they need, one piece at a time.

Can parents and fans order too?

Yes. Anyone with the store link can order their own sizes, so families and fans get gear without the coach collecting money or forms.

Can we get jerseys with our team name, player numbers, and a sponsor logo?

Yes — as a custom team order with your account executive rather than through the webstore, since each jersey is personalized. Your AE handles the roster, names, numbers, and sponsor logo placement, and we can run a webstore alongside it for the hoodies, caps, and fan gear everyone orders themselves. Call 253-922-5793 to start both.

Can we open the store just for the season?

Yes. Many teams run a store for a set window each season, then close it, or keep one open year-round.

Ready to set up your team's store?

Tell us about your team, club, or league and we'll build a store with the gear your roster wears, all under your logo. Same family-owned Milton shop since 1977.
