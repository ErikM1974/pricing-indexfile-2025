Fundraising webstore FAQ

What is a fundraising apparel store?

It's an online store we build with your logo, so supporters order their own tees, hoodies, and caps and every order supports your cause. We embroider it and ship it — no inventory to buy and no order forms to collect.

How is pricing calculated?

Each item is priced at our first-tier (1–7 piece) left-chest embroidery rate plus a flat $6 handling fee — everyone pays the same fair per-piece rate, with no group minimums and no inventory to buy up front. There's a one-time $300 setup fee to build and brand your store. People order what they need, one piece at a time.

How does our group earn from it?

We set a price that includes your fundraising margin, so every order raises money for your cause. Tell us your goal and we'll build it in.

Can we run it for just a campaign?

Yes. Open the store for a two-week campaign, then close it, or keep one open year-round.

What do you recommend for a company organizing shirts for a community event?

A limited-run event store works best: we open it for a set window, participants order and pay for their own shirts, your fundraising margin is built into each price, and orders ship before event day. Anything personalized — like jerseys with names, numbers, or a sponsor logo — your account executive handles as a companion order. Call 253-922-5793 to plan your event.
