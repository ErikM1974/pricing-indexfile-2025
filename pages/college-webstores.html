Need printed shirts instead of embroidery? We offer DTG (direct-to-garment) printing on our tested and approved cotton tees and hoodies — DTG ink is made for cotton, so print stores stick to cotton styles we know print beautifully. Polyester and performance gear can be printed too: we use DTF transfers, printed by our Los Angeles partner and heat-pressed on every shirt right here in our Milton shop. Transfers are produced out of house, so they add a little lead time — and we ask that a transfer store expects to sell at least 10 printed pieces.

College webstore FAQ

What is a college or club apparel store?

It's an online store we build with your logo, so members order their own hoodies, tees, and caps on demand. We embroider it and ship it, so you never stock inventory or collect money at meetings.

How is pricing calculated?

Each item is priced at our first-tier (1–7 piece) left-chest embroidery rate plus a flat $6 handling fee — everyone pays the same fair per-piece rate, with no group minimums and no inventory to buy up front. There's a one-time $300 setup fee to build and brand your store. People order what they need, one piece at a time, and most orders are embroidered and shipped within about two weeks.

Can each member order and ship to themselves?

Yes. Every member orders their own sizes, and we can ship to each person or to the house or club.

Can we open it for rush or an event only?

Yes. Open the store for rush or a specific event, then close it, or keep one open all year.

Ready to set up your chapter's store?

Tell us about your chapter or club and we'll build a store with the gear your members wear, all under your logo. Same family-owned Milton shop since 1977.
