How we decorate Port & Company

Screen print for volume

At big-order counts, screen printing is the value play — bold front logos, full backs, and a per-piece price that keeps dropping the more you order. Port & Company's smooth cotton and blends hold crisp ink.

DTF for full color

Full-color transfers make photo art, gradients, and multi-design runs easy — ideal for a fundraiser with several designs or a short event drop, with no per-color screen fees.

Embroidery on fleece

The Core Fleece hoodies and crews take a clean embroidered logo when you want a more finished look than print — a nice step up for staff and team pieces.

How a Port & Company order works

  1. Send your design and a rough count through the quote form — takes two minutes.
  2. We recommend a method (screen print, DTF, or embroidery) and quote it same-day with real decorated pricing.
  3. You approve a proof — you see the art on the garment before we print a thing.
  4. Gear ships or gets picked up — typically about two weeks from proof approval, rush available.

Port & Company prices best at two dozen and up; smaller runs carry a small-batch fee, always quoted up front. Reorders keep your art on file, so the next run is one email.

Custom Port & Company FAQ

Why is Port & Company so budget-friendly?
Port & Company is the value-basics line — proven tees and fleece at the sharpest per-piece price we carry. There's no premium-label markup, so a big order, a school fundraiser, or an event giveaway stays in budget while still looking and feeling good.
What's the difference between the Core Cotton and Core Blend tees?
The Core Cotton Tee (PC54) is 100% cotton — a soft, classic hand that's the go-to for screen printing. The Core Blend Tee (PC55) is a 50/50 cotton-poly blend that resists shrinking and wrinkles a little better and runs a touch smoother. Both print great; cotton for feel, blend for durability.
Is Port & Company good for big orders and giveaways?
It's the brand we reach for on exactly those. The value price scales, so fundraisers, company picnics, event staff, and volume giveaways come in on budget. The PC54 Core Cotton Tee is the single most-printed style we run — thousands a year.
Do you have women's and youth Port & Company sizes?
Yes — Port & Company runs women's cuts and a full youth line, including a youth Core Cotton Tee, plus long-sleeve, tall, and pocket options. A whole family or a K-12 school can match in one order.
Is there a minimum order?
Pricing is sharpest at two dozen and up, which is where most big-order and event runs land. Smaller runs carry a small-batch fee that's quoted up front. Every quote shows the real number before anything goes to production.
