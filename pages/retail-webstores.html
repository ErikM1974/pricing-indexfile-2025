Need printed shirts instead of embroidery? We offer DTG (direct-to-garment) printing on our tested and approved cotton tees and hoodies — DTG ink is made for cotton, so print stores stick to cotton styles we know print beautifully. Polyester and performance gear can be printed too: we use DTF transfers, printed by our specialty transfer partner in Los Angeles and heat-pressed on every shirt right here in our Milton shop. Transfers are produced out of house, so they add a little lead time — and we ask that a transfer store expects to sell at least 12 printed pieces.

Retail webstore FAQ

What is a retail apparel store?

It's an online store we build with your logo, so your store crew orders their own tees, hoodies, and caps on demand. We embroider it and ship it, so you never stock inventory or collect sizes.

How is pricing calculated?

Each item is priced at our first-tier (1–7 piece) left-chest embroidery rate plus a flat $6 handling fee — everyone pays the same fair per-piece rate, with no group minimums and no inventory to buy up front. There's a one-time $300 setup fee to build and brand your store. People order what they need, one piece at a time, and most orders are embroidered and shipped within about two weeks.

Can we sell merch to customers in the same store?

Yes. We can add a merch section your regulars can buy from, right alongside your staff apparel, all under your brand.

Can each location order on its own?

Yes. Everyone orders their own sizes, and we can ship to one store or drop-ship to each location, great for multi-store businesses.

Ready to set up your store's apparel shop?

Tell us about your business and we'll build a store with the staff apparel and merch your crew wears, all under your logo. Same family-owned Milton shop since 1977.
