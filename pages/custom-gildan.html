How we decorate Gildan

Screen print for volume

This is where Gildan shines — bold front logos and full backs at a per-piece price that keeps dropping the more you order. The smooth cotton and blends hold crisp ink through hundreds of prints.

DTF for full color

Full-color transfers make photo art, gradients, and multi-design runs easy — great for a fundraiser with several designs or a short event drop, with no per-color screen fees.

Embroidery on fleece

The Heavy Blend hoodies and crews take a clean embroidered logo when you want a more finished look than print — a nice step up for staff and team pieces.

How a Gildan order works

  1. Send your design and a rough count through the quote form — takes two minutes.
  2. We recommend a method (screen print or DTF) and quote it same-day with real decorated pricing.
  3. You approve a proof — you see the art on the garment before we print a thing.
  4. Gear ships or gets picked up — typically about two weeks from proof approval, rush available.

Gildan prices best at two dozen and up; smaller runs carry a small-batch fee, always quoted up front. Reorders keep your art on file, so the next run is one email.

Custom Gildan FAQ

Why is Gildan the go-to for big print runs?
Gildan is the industry-standard blank — the lowest per-piece price we carry, the widest color range, and a smooth surface that screen prints clean. When you need hundreds of shirts for a fundraiser, event, or giveaway and the budget is the deciding factor, this is the brand.
What's the difference between Ultra Cotton, DryBlend, and Heavy Blend?
Ultra Cotton (2000) is a heavier 100% cotton tee — the classic print surface. DryBlend (8000) is a 50/50 cotton-poly that resists shrinking and wrinkles and dries faster. Heavy Blend is the 50/50 fleece used in the hoodies and crews (18500/18000). Softstyle (64000) is a lighter, softer ring-spun tee if you want a more fashion feel.
Is Gildan good for fundraisers and events?
It's the brand we reach for on exactly those. The value price scales, so school fundraisers, 5Ks, festivals, and volume giveaways come in on budget, and the huge color range means you can match any team or event palette. Screen printing gets even cheaper per piece as the count climbs.
Do you have youth, tall, and long-sleeve Gildan?
Yes — Gildan runs a deep line: youth tees and hoodies, tall tees, long-sleeve tees, pocket tees, and crewneck sweatshirts, mostly in unisex sizing. A whole family, a K-12 school, or an event of every size can match in one order.
Is there a minimum order?
Pricing is sharpest at two dozen and up, which is where most big-order and event runs land. Smaller runs carry a small-batch fee that's quoted up front. Every quote shows the real number before anything goes to production.
