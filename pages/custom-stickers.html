      Questions

      What's the minimum order?

      50 stickers — $87 for a 2 × 2 or $98 for a 3 × 3. Need fewer? Call (253) 922-5793 and we'll tell you straight away whether we can do it.

      Why do you price by square sizes?

      We price to the next standard square. A 2 × 3 design prices at our 3 × 3 tier — and we still cut it at your exact 2 × 3 shape. Since you're already on that tier, you can go up to a full 3 × 3 at no extra cost.

      What's the art setup fee?

      It covers your mockup, a print-readiness check on the die line, and up to 2 rounds of revisions. It applies once per new design — including when you send print-ready vector art. Every reorder of that same design is $0 setup, forever.

      How long does it take?

      10–12 business days from artwork approval. The clock starts when you approve your proof, not when you order — and your rep usually turns a proof around the same day. Need it faster? Rush production is available — ask your rep.

      What are they made of?

      ORAJET 3651 by ORAFOL — 2.75 mil calendered PVC, white film, permanent solvent adhesive, rated up to 5 years outdoors, UL 969 / CSA C22.2 No. 015 listed. Printed on a Roland TrueVIS LG-540 with 7-colour ECO-UV ink at 1440 dpi and machine contour cut.

      What about stickers bigger than 6 inches?

      Over 6 inches we print large-format decals, priced by the square foot, up to 52 inches wide. Same vinyl, same printer.

      Do you print banners?

      Yes — 13 oz Ultraflex Ultima Pro FL front-lit matte, sewn hems and #2 brass grommets every 24 inches included, $10 per square foot, $40 minimum. Any size.

      Is sales tax included?

      No. Washington sales tax is added on your invoice.

      How we size stickers

      You don't have to be exact. We print five standard squares — 2 × 2, 3 × 3, 4 × 4, 5 × 5 and 6 × 6 inches — and we price to the next square your design fits inside.

      So a 2 × 3 inch design prices at our 3 × 3 tier. We still cut it at your exact 2 × 3 shape. You're not paying for a bigger sticker — you're paying our 3 × 3 rate, because that's the tier your artwork falls into.

      And since you're already on that tier, you can go up to a full 3 × 3 at no extra cost. Plenty of people do.

      Odd shapes price by the bounding box — the smallest rectangle the art fits inside. A 10″ circle is a 10 × 10.

      Your rep confirms the exact size and the final price on your proof — before anything is printed or charged. If the artwork you send changes which tier you're on, you'll see it on the proof and you can say no.

      Quantities work the same way. We price at the next break — enter 250 and you'll be quoted our 300 tier.
