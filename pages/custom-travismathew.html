How a TravisMathew order works

  1. Send your logo and a rough count through the quote form — takes two minutes.
  2. We recommend the pieces and colors and quote it same-day with real decorated pricing.
  3. You approve a proof — including exact embroidery placement on the polo or cap.
  4. Gear ships or gets picked up — typically about two weeks from proof approval, rush available.

TravisMathew works for smaller premium runs and larger orders alike; smaller quantities carry a small-batch fee, always quoted up front. Reorders keep your logo on file, so next year's outing is one email.

Custom TravisMathew FAQ

Is TravisMathew good for golf tournaments and company outings?
It's a favorite for exactly that. TravisMathew has a pro-shop, resort feel that makes a tournament gift or an outing polo feel like a real reward — the kind of piece players keep wearing after the round. It's the move when you want the golf apparel to impress.
What makes TravisMathew polos different?
TravisMathew builds a soft performance polo with a relaxed-premium fit and styling — solids, chest stripes, and textured slub knits — that reads more country-club than corporate uniform. It moves and breathes for a full round and still looks sharp at the 19th hole.
Can you embroider TravisMathew gear?
Yes — a left-chest embroidered logo is the standard treatment on the polos and 1/4-zips, and the caps take a crisp front embroidery. We digitize your artwork in-house so it sits clean on the premium fabric.
Do you offer women's TravisMathew styles?
Yes — TravisMathew makes women's styles like the Crestview 1/4-zip (TM1WW003), so a mixed foursome or a whole company outing can match. We'll pull the women's version wherever it's offered.
Is there a minimum order?
TravisMathew works well for smaller premium runs as well as larger orders; smaller quantities carry a small-batch fee that's quoted up front. Pricing is sharpest at two dozen and up. Every quote shows the real number before anything goes to production.
