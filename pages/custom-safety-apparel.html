Common Questions

What are custom safety stripes?

They're bold high-visibility stripes we screen-print across the front and back of hi-vis apparel, usually with your company name or logo worked in. They make a crew stand out on the job — and put your brand on the gear at the same time — at a fraction of the cost of buying certified reflective garments for everyone.

Do safety stripe shirts meet ANSI requirements?

A screen-printed hi-vis tee or hoodie is high-visibility work apparel, not a certified ANSI/ISEA 107 garment. If your job site requires certified hi-vis, we also carry certified options like the CornerStone ANSI 107 Class 2 mesh-back safety vest (CSV405) — tell us the requirement and we'll match it. For crews that just need to be seen and branded, the safety-stripe shirts and hoodies are the value pick.

Can I pick a stripe design or get a custom one?

Both. Choose from our stripe layouts — Standard, Construction Zone, Diamond Plate, Warning, back-center logo, and more — or our in-house graphic artist Steve will design custom stripes for your crew with your logo, colors, and text. Every design gets a proof before we print.

What apparel can you print safety stripes on?

Hi-vis tees, long-sleeves, and hoodies in safety green, orange, and yellow, plus safety vests. Our top sellers are the Port & Company, Gildan, and Jerzees hi-vis blanks — shown below — but we can print stripes on most hi-vis styles you like.

Is there a minimum order for custom safety stripes?

Screen printing prices best at two dozen and up, where most crew orders land. Smaller runs carry a small-batch fee that's quoted up front. Every quote shows the real number before anything goes to production.
