How we decorate Port Authority

Embroidery (the standard)

A left-chest embroidered logo is the classic company look on Port Authority polos, jackets, and woven shirts, and the caps take a crisp front embroidery. We digitize your artwork in-house so it sits sharp on every piece.

Caps, done right

Structured caps like the C112 hold a bold, dimensional logo; the CP90 knit cap takes a tight cuff embroidery. Between them they anchor more of our orders than any other cap on the board.

A coordinated program

Because it all comes from one brand, you can build a matched set — cap, polo, soft shell — in the same color family, then reorder any piece later without hunting for a match.

How a Port Authority order works

  1. Send your logo and a rough count through the quote form — takes two minutes.
  2. We recommend the pieces and method and quote it same-day with real decorated pricing.
  3. You approve a proof — including exact embroidery placement on caps, polos, and jackets.
  4. Gear ships or gets picked up — typically about two weeks from proof approval, rush available.

Port Authority prices best at two dozen and up; smaller runs carry a small-batch fee, always quoted up front. Reorders keep your logo on file, so restocking is one email.

Custom Port Authority FAQ

Is Port Authority good for company uniforms and employee apparel?
It's built for it. Port Authority is the everyday corporate-apparel line — caps, polos, soft shells, and woven shirts that cover a whole company's branded wardrobe from one brand, at a price that makes reorders and new-hire kits easy. It's the most-ordered brand we carry.
What are your most popular Port Authority caps?
The C112 snapback trucker and the CP90 knit cap are our two most-embroidered Port Authority caps by a wide margin — the trucker for year-round crews and events, the knit cap for cold-weather gear. Both take a crisp embroidered logo.
What's the difference between the SuperPro and Silk Touch polos?
The SuperPro React (K810) is the durable easy-care polo — snag-resistant and stain-release treated, so it holds up to daily wear and washing. The Silk Touch (K500) is the classic soft-pique company polo with a smooth, dressier hand. Both are 65/35 poly-cotton pique and embroider cleanly; it comes down to rugged easy-care versus soft classic.
Do you offer women's Port Authority styles?
Yes — Port Authority runs a full women's line (the L-prefix styles, like the LK810 SuperPro polo) alongside the men's cuts, so a whole team can match. We'll pull the right fit for everyone on your list.
Is there a minimum order?
Pricing is sharpest at two dozen and up, where most company-apparel orders land. Smaller runs carry a small-batch fee that's quoted up front. Every quote shows the real number before anything goes to production.
