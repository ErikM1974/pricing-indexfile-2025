    Questions

    What's included in the price?

    Full-colour print on 13 oz Ultraflex Ultima Pro front-lit matte vinyl, sewn hems all the way round, and #2 brass grommets every 24 inches. That's the finished banner — hems and grommets aren't extras.

    Is there a minimum?

    $40 per banner. Because of that, every banner up to about 4 square feet (2 × 2 ft) costs the same $40 — going smaller than that doesn't reduce the price.

    Do I get a discount for ordering several?

    Not on the banner itself — banners are priced by the square foot, so ten cost ten times one. Where you save is size and setup: one art setup covers the whole order, however many you print.

    How big can you go?

    Any length. Our roll prints up to 52 inches wide, so a banner whose shorter side is over 52 inches is seamed from panels — still one banner, and we'll tell you before we print.

    How long does it take?

    10–12 business days from artwork approval. The clock starts when you approve your proof, not when you order. Need it faster? Rush production is available — ask your rep.

    Can you do pole pockets instead of grommets?

    Yes — pole pockets are priced by the linear foot and can go top, bottom or both. Tick the box above and your quote shows them itemised.

    Is sales tax included?

    No. Washington sales tax is added on your invoice.

    Picking a banner size

    Banners are priced by the square foot — $10 per square foot, with a $40 minimum per banner. A 3 × 6 ft banner is 18 square feet, so it's $180.

    Any size works. The sizes listed are the ones people ask for most, but enter whatever you need and we'll price it.

    Under about 4 square feet the $40 minimum takes over, so a 2 × 2 ft banner and a 1 × 1 ft banner both cost $40. If you're that small, go bigger — it's free.
