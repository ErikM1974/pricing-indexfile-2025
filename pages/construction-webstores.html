Construction webstore FAQ

What is a construction company apparel store?

It's an online store we build with your logo and a curated set of workwear, so your crew orders their own Carhartt, hi-vis, polos, and caps on demand. We decorate and ship it, so you never stock inventory or collect sizes.

Can we add hi-vis and safety gear to the store?

Yes. We add screen-printed hi-vis apparel and ANSI-certified safety vests right alongside your Carhartt and polos, all under your logo.

Can each job site or new hire order on their own?

Yes. Everyone orders their own sizes and we can ship to one office or drop-ship to each person or site — ideal as crews grow and change.

Is there a minimum order?

It depends on the store type. On-demand stores let the crew order one item at a time, while scheduled open-and-close stores may have a group minimum. We match the setup to your crew.

Ready to set up your crew's store?

Tell us about your construction company and we'll build a store with the workwear your crew wears — Carhartt, hi-vis, polos, and caps — all under your logo. Same family-owned Milton shop since 1977.
