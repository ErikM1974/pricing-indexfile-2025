Restaurant webstore FAQ

What is a restaurant apparel store?

It's an online store we build with your logo, so your restaurant, bar, or cafe staff order their own tees, polos, and caps on demand. We embroider it and ship it, so you never stock inventory or collect sizes.

How is pricing calculated?

Each item is priced at our first-tier (1–7 piece) left-chest embroidery rate plus a flat $6 handling fee — everyone pays the same fair per-piece rate, with no group minimums and no inventory to buy up front. There's a one-time $300 setup fee to build and brand your store. Staff order what they need, one piece at a time.

Can each location order on its own?

Yes. Every location or staff member orders their own sizes, and we can ship to one spot or drop-ship to each restaurant — great for multi-location groups.

Do you make aprons and kitchen wear too?

Yes, we produce custom aprons and kitchen wear alongside your staff apparel. Ask us for a quote and we'll add them to your store or order.

Ready to set up your restaurant's store?

Tell us about your restaurant, bar, or cafe and we'll build a store with the staff apparel your team wears — embroidered tees, polos, and caps — all under your logo. Same family-owned Milton shop since 1977.
