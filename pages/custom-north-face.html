How a North Face order works

  1. Send your logo and a rough count through the quote form — takes two minutes.
  2. We recommend the pieces and colors and quote it same-day with real decorated pricing.
  3. You approve a proof — including exact embroidery placement on the jacket or fleece.
  4. Gear ships or gets picked up — typically about two weeks from proof approval, rush available.

North Face works for smaller premium runs and larger orders alike; smaller quantities carry a small-batch fee, always quoted up front. Reorders keep your logo on file, so round two is one email.

Custom The North Face FAQ

Can you embroider The North Face jackets with our logo?
Yes — a left-chest embroidered logo is the standard treatment on The North Face jackets, fleece, and vests, and it's what makes a premium branded gift. We digitize your artwork in-house so it sits clean on the technical fabric.
Why is The North Face worth it for company gifts?
It's a logo everyone recognizes and actually wants to wear. A North Face jacket with your mark reads as a real gift, not swag — the kind of piece that gets worn for years, keeping your brand in view long after a cheaper giveaway would be gone.
What's the difference between DryVent and soft shell?
DryVent is The North Face's waterproof, breathable rain protection — the go-to for wet weather. The soft shells are wind-resistant and water-repellent with a softer hand, better for cool, dry days and everyday layering. We'll steer you to the right one for your climate.
Do you offer women's North Face styles?
Yes — The North Face makes women's cuts of the DryVent rain jacket, Skyline fleece, and Everyday insulated vest, so a whole team can match in the right fit. We'll pull the women's version wherever it's offered.
Is there a minimum order?
North Face works well for smaller premium runs as well as larger orders; smaller quantities carry a small-batch fee that's quoted up front. Pricing is sharpest at two dozen and up. Every quote shows the real number before anything goes to production.
