How pricing works: each price includes your left-chest embroidered logo at our first-tier (1–7 piece) rate plus a flat $6 handling fee — no order minimums and no inventory to buy up front. A one-time $300 setup fee builds and brands your store. Prices shown are examples; your exact store pricing is set when we build it.

Need printed shirts instead of embroidery? We offer DTG (direct-to-garment) printing on our tested and approved cotton tees and hoodies — DTG ink is made for cotton, so print stores stick to cotton styles we know print beautifully. Polyester and performance gear can be printed too: we use DTF transfers, printed by our Los Angeles partner and heat-pressed on every shirt right here in our Milton shop. Transfers are produced out of house, so they add a little lead time — and we ask that a transfer store expects to sell at least 10 printed pieces. Print-option prices shown on each item are full-front prints and include the same $6 handling fee.

Industrial webstore FAQ

What is an industrial apparel store?

It's an online store we build with your logo, so your plant and field teams order their own workwear on demand. We embroider it and ship it, so you never stock inventory or collect sizes.

How is pricing calculated?

Each item is priced at our first-tier (1–7 piece) left-chest embroidery rate plus a flat $6 handling fee — everyone pays the same fair per-piece rate, with no group minimums and no inventory to buy up front. There's a one-time $300 setup fee to build and brand your store. People order what they need, one piece at a time, and most orders are embroidered and shipped within about two weeks.

Can each site or new hire order on their own?

Yes. Everyone orders their own sizes, and we can ship to one plant or drop-ship to each site, ideal as teams grow and change.

Can we offer premium layers for management and basics for the floor?

Yes. We curate the store so management sees soft shells and quarter-zips while the floor sees durable Carhartt and work tees, all under your logo.

Ready to set up your plant's store?

Tell us about your operation and we'll build a store with the workwear your teams wear, all under your logo. Same family-owned Milton shop since 1977.
