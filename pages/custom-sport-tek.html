Why teams choose Sport-Tek

Performance that keeps up

Sport-Wick fabric pulls sweat off the skin and dries fast; PosiCharge locks in color so team names and numbers stay vivid and resist fading through a season of washes. It's real athletic wear, not a cotton tee with a sporty name — the kind of thing a player is glad to wear off the field too.

Outfit everyone

A full men's, women's, and youth range in sizes XS through 4XL means a co-ed league, a whole school, or a family all match. And because Sport-Tek is the value performance brand, you can cover a big roster without the premium-label price tag.

Built to decorate

Polos and fleece take a crisp embroidered logo; tees and jerseys are a natural stage for screen-printed or DTF names and numbers. However your team wants its mark, Sport-Tek is an easy, forgiving canvas — and we do every method in-house.

How we decorate Sport-Tek

Embroidery on polos & fleece

A left-chest embroidered logo is the standard look for team polos, 1/4-zips, and jackets. We digitize your artwork in-house so it sits clean on the performance knit — sharp, durable, and washproof.

Screen print for team tees

At team volume, screen printing is the value play for Competitor tees and jerseys — bold front logos, big backs, and a per-piece price that drops the more you order.

DTF for names & numbers

Full-color transfers make individual player names, numbers, and multi-color logos easy — perfect for a roster where every jersey is a little different. No screen fees per name.

How a Sport-Tek order works

  1. Send your logo and a rough roster through the quote form — takes two minutes.
  2. We recommend a method (embroidery, screen print, or DTF) and quote it same-day with real decorated pricing.
  3. You approve a proof — including a names-and-numbers layout for team orders.
  4. Gear ships or gets picked up — typically about two weeks from proof approval, rush available.

Sport-Tek prices best at two dozen and up; smaller runs carry a small-batch fee, always quoted up front. Reorders keep your logo and roster on file, so next season is one email.

Custom Sport-Tek FAQ

Is Sport-Tek good for team uniforms and spirit wear?
It's built for exactly that — the value-priced performance line schools, leagues, gyms, and rec teams reach for. Moisture-wicking polos and tees, 1/4-zips, and hoodies in men's, women's, and youth cuts, sizes XS through 4XL, so you can outfit a whole roster in matching gear.
What's the difference between Sport-Wick and PosiCharge?
Sport-Wick is the moisture-wicking fabric that pulls sweat off the skin so it dries fast. PosiCharge is a color-lock technology that keeps printed names, numbers, and logos from fading through hard practice wear and repeated washing. Many Sport-Tek styles have both.
Can you print player names and numbers?
Yes — we handle full rosters with screen printing or DTF transfers for individual names and numbers, plus embroidery for logos on polos and fleece. Send your roster and we'll lay it out in your team colors.
Do you have women's and youth Sport-Tek sizes?
Yes — a full women's line (the LST styles) and a deep youth line (YST) run alongside the men's cuts, so a family, a school, or a co-ed league can all match.
Is there a minimum order?
Pricing is sharpest at two dozen and up, where most team and spirit-wear orders land. Smaller runs carry a small-batch fee that's quoted up front. Every quote shows the real number before anything goes to production.
