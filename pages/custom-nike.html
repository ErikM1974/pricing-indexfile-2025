How we decorate Nike

Embroidery (the standard)

A left-chest embroidered logo, opposite the swoosh, is the classic Nike treatment — we digitize your artwork in-house so it sits crisp on the fine Dri-FIT pique. Names, numbers, and monograms too.

The swoosh is included

Every Nike piece ships with the swoosh already on it. Your logo goes alongside — the swoosh signals quality, your logo does the branding. Two marks, one sharp result.

Matched programs

Custom Nike FAQ

Can you embroider a company logo on Nike polos?
Yes — embroidery is the standard treatment. We stitch your logo on the left chest, opposite the swoosh, and digitize the artwork in-house so it sits crisp on the Dri-FIT pique. It's our most-requested premium polo program.
Does the Nike swoosh come on the polo already?
Yes — every Nike garment ships with the swoosh on it. Your embroidered logo goes alongside it: the swoosh does the quality signaling, your logo does the branding.
Why choose Nike over a standard polo?
When the polo is a client gift, a golf-day piece, or a management order, the swoosh carries weight a no-name polo can't. It's the tier that says a company invested — and people keep and wear it.
Do you carry women's Nike polos?
Yes — the women's Dri-FIT Micro Pique 2.0 (NKDC1991) pairs with the unisex NKDC1963, so a whole-team program fits everyone with matching embroidery.
How long does an order take?
Typically about two weeks from proof approval, with rush options available. You always approve a stitched proof first.
