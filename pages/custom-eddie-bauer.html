How we decorate Eddie Bauer

Left-chest embroidery

The classic, understated treatment — a crisp embroidered logo on the left chest of a soft shell, rain jacket, or vest. We digitize your artwork in-house so it sits clean and premium on the technical fabric.

Branded blankets & gifts

The quilted fleece blanket takes an embroidered logo for a memorable, high-perceived-value gift — a client thank-you or milestone piece that stands apart from the usual swag.

A coordinated program

How an Eddie Bauer order works

  1. Send your logo and a rough count through the quote form — takes two minutes.
  2. We recommend the pieces and colors and quote it same-day with real decorated pricing.
  3. You approve a proof — including exact embroidery placement on the jacket or vest.
  4. Gear ships or gets picked up — typically about two weeks from proof approval, rush available.

Eddie Bauer works for smaller premium runs and larger orders alike; smaller quantities carry a small-batch fee, always quoted up front. Reorders keep your logo on file, so round two is one email.

Custom Eddie Bauer FAQ

Is Eddie Bauer good for executive gifts and corporate outerwear?
It's a go-to for exactly that. Eddie Bauer is outdoor-heritage outerwear with a refined, retail feel — a soft shell or quilted jacket with your logo reads as a considered gift, not a giveaway. It's a favorite for leadership apparel, client gifts, and cold-weather crews who expect quality.
What's the difference between a soft shell and a quilted jacket?
A soft shell (like the EB532) is a bonded, wind- and water-resistant layer for cool, active days — breathable and not bulky. A quilted jacket (like the EB510) adds insulation for real cold. We'll match the jacket to your climate and how the crew will wear it.
Can you embroider Eddie Bauer jackets?
Yes — a left-chest embroidered logo is the standard treatment on Eddie Bauer soft shells, rain jackets, quilted jackets, and vests. We digitize your artwork in-house so it sits clean and premium on the technical fabric.
Do you offer women's Eddie Bauer styles?
Yes — Eddie Bauer makes women's cuts like the packable wind jacket (EB501) and the quilted jacket (EB511), so a whole team can match in the right fit. We'll pull the women's version wherever it's offered.
Is there a minimum order?
Eddie Bauer works well for smaller premium runs as well as larger orders; smaller quantities carry a small-batch fee that's quoted up front. Pricing is sharpest at two dozen and up. Every quote shows the real number before anything goes to production.
