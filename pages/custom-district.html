How we decorate District

Screen print for merch runs

At volume, screen printing is the value play for District tees — bold front logos, big backs, and a per-piece price that drops the more you order. The smooth knit holds crisp edges.

DTF for full color

Full-color transfers make photographic art, gradients, and small runs easy — perfect for a merch line with multiple designs or a short event drop. No screen fees per color.

Embroidery on fleece & caps

The V.I.T. hoodies and the trucker cap take a clean embroidered logo when you want a more finished, premium look than print — an easy add to any District order.

How a District order works

  1. Send your design and a rough count through the quote form — takes two minutes.
  2. We recommend a method (screen print, DTF, or embroidery) and quote it same-day with real decorated pricing.
  3. You approve a proof — you see the art on the garment before we print a thing.
  4. Gear ships or gets picked up — typically about two weeks from proof approval, rush available.

District prices best at two dozen and up; smaller runs carry a small-batch fee, always quoted up front. Reorders keep your art on file, so the next drop is one email.

Custom District FAQ

Why are District tees so soft?
District builds its tees from ring-spun cotton and tri-blend knits, which have a smoother, broken-in hand right out of the box. The Very Important Tee and Perfect Tri lines feel like a favorite shirt someone's owned for years — that's why they get worn instead of stuffed in a drawer.
What's the difference between the Very Important Tee and the Perfect Tri?
The Very Important Tee (DT6000) is a soft ring-spun cotton tee at a value price — the everyday workhorse. The Perfect Tri (DM130) is a 50/25/25 blend of polyester, cotton, and rayon with a heathered look and a lighter, drapier feel. Both print beautifully; the Tri just leans more fashion.
Is District good for retail merch and events?
Very. The retail-soft hand and fashion fits make District a favorite for merch you actually sell, event giveaways people keep, and casual brand wear — the kind of shirt customers put on by choice, not obligation.
Do you have women's and youth District sizes?
Yes — District runs women's cuts (tanks, v-necks, and rocker tees) and a youth line, including a youth Very Important Tee, so a family, a school, or a whole event can match. We'll pull the right fit for everyone.
Is there a minimum order?
Pricing is sharpest at two dozen and up, which is where most merch and event runs land. Smaller runs carry a small-batch fee that's quoted up front. Every quote shows the real number before anything goes to production.
