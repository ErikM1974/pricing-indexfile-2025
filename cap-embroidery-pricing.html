Cap Embroidery FAQs

What is the maximum size for cap embroidery?

Standard cap embroidery areas are typically up to 2.5" x 5" for the front panel. Side and back embroidery may have different size limitations.

Can you embroider on all types of caps?

We can embroider on most cap styles including structured, unstructured, fitted, snapback, and trucker caps. Some materials may require special handling.

Do you offer 3D embroidery for caps?

Yes, we offer 3D puff embroidery for caps at an additional charge. This creates a raised, dimensional effect that's popular for cap designs.
