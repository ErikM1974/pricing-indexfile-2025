DTG Header Update Test

To test the header updates:

  1. Open the DTG pricing page
  2. The header should show:
    • Contact bar with phone and email
    • Logo and navigation area
    • Pricing context bar with "DTG Pricing Calculator"
    • Live pricing display showing:
      • Qty: 24 (default)
      • Per Shirt: $0.00 (updates with pricing)
  3. When you change quantity in step 2, the header Qty should update
  4. When you select a location in step 1, the Per Shirt price should update
  5. The header should be fixed at the top when scrolling

Expected Behavior:

  • Initial state: Qty: 24, Per Shirt: $0.00
  • After location selection: Qty: 24, Per Shirt: (actual price, e.g., $18.00)
  • After quantity change: Qty: (new quantity), Per Shirt: (updated price)
