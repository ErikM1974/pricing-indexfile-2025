# Diego Garcia MWR Apparel for Military Comfort

Northwest Custom Apparel has been providing decorated apparel to the US Navy in Diego Garcia since 1999. This beautiful island, located 7 degrees north of the equator and directly south of India, is home to a Morale, Welfare, and Recreation (MWR). This program offers various services and activities to enhance the quality of life for military personnel and their families stationed on the island. In light of this, Northwest Custom Apparel has created a range of products tailored to the people of Diego Garcia – from bar coasters to caps and shirts. We have designed and produced thousands of hats with unique designs specifically for the Diego Garcia Yacht Club.
