# NW Custom Apparel’s Legacy with the US Navy in Diego Garcia: A Case Study

## NW Custom Apparel’s Ongoing Partnership with Diego Garcia

Since 1999, Northwest Custom Apparel has proudly served the US Navy stationed on the remote island of Diego Garcia. Located strategically in the Indian Ocean, Diego Garcia hosts military personnel who benefit from NW Custom Apparel's custom designs, bringing comfort and camaraderie to life on the island.

## Project Highlights and Services

![T3717 yacht club flags](https://nwcustomapparel.net/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/T3717-yacht-club-flags-300x173-1-1.webp)

NW Custom Apparel supplies a range of personalized items, from bar coasters and caps to embroidered shirts, designed for various island establishments. The **Diego Garcia Yacht Club** is a prominent client, with customized caps featuring unique island-themed embroidery, a perfect keepsake for the club’s members. This is the design which has been applied to thousands of caps for the Diego Garcia Yacht Club.

![p2462 goat locker](https://nwcustomapparel.net/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/p2462-goat-locker-300x188-1-1.webp)

Another project we have done was [make embroidered shirts](https://nwcustomapparel.net/embroidery-on-shirts-near-me/) and caps for the **Navy Chiefs’ quarters**, known as the "Goat Locker." The quarters are a cherished place for Navy Chiefs, symbolized by the embroidered anchor insignias reflecting different Chief ranks, such as Chief, Senior Chief, and Master Chief. The anchors with the starts are images of the ranks of the Chief Petty Officers. No star is a chief, one star is a senior chief, two start is master chief.

### Enhancing Morale and Recreation

![T3217](https://nwcustomapparel.net/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/j550-300x194-1-1.webp)

The **Morale, Welfare, and Recreation (MWR)** unit, an essential group on Diego Garcia, collaborates with NW Custom Apparel to provide [MWR's custom apparel](https://nwcustomapparel.net/diego-garcia-mwr-hats-and-shirts/) that supports their efforts. The apparel enhances activities, making them more memorable for islanders and creating a sense of belonging.

The Moral Welfare is the group which makes this island enjoyable because they manage all the clubs, athletics, personal services, and recreation. It is one of the most loved units on the island. By the way haircuts, laundry massages are all free.

![j550](https://nwcustomapparel.net/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/25375-yacht-club-300x256-1-1.webp)

A very popular place to hang out is the yacht club. We have done designs for the yacht club which islanders can purchase and send home to remember their stay on the island. The yacht club has a bar (of course) and their membership is recognized by all the yacht clubs in the world. It pays to join especially if you are traveling.

![25375 yacht club](https://nwcustomapparel.net/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/M2004-The-doge-300x229-1-1.webp)

The island is owned by the British and as you know the Brits are famous for their clubs. Where ever their troops are stationed you'll find a well stocked pub. There is a excellent British club on the island.

If you ever get to Diego Garcia this place is highly recommended. You must stop by and visit their gift store with all the neat British stuff.

### Memorable Merchandise

![T3217](https://nwcustomapparel.net/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/T3217-300x250-1-1-1.webp)

The island is often referred to as Dodge City. The island’s **gift shops** stock items custom-designed by NW Custom Apparel, including hats featuring the famous Dodge City donkeys—a fun nod to the island's unique wildlife and charm. These items are popular with residents and make perfect souvenirs.

## Conclusion

[NW Custom Apparel](https://nwcustomapparel.net/)’s collaboration with Diego Garcia showcases its commitment to delivering high-quality, personalized apparel for unique communities worldwide. This partnership underscores how custom apparel builds morale and provides a connection to home, even in remote locations.
