# Erik Mickelson: Elevating Apparel Design 2024

Hello, I'm Erik Mickelson, and for over 26 years, I've dedicated myself to the art of graphic design in the custom apparel industry. At Northwest Custom Apparel, my team and I are embarking on an exciting path in 2024, where we aim to elevate our services to new heights and bring unparalleled value to our clients.

## **My Philosophy in Design:**

Graphic design is more than my profession; it's my passion. Over the years, I've learned that combining creativity with practicality is essential to creating good designs that resonate with our clients' visions. It's about understanding the story behind each project and bringing it to life through apparel.

## **2024: A New Chapter at Northwest Custom Apparel**

This year, I'm particularly excited to introduce several enhancements to our services:

- **Tiered Mockup Packages**: Understanding each project's uniqueness, I've developed a tiered approach to our mockup services. Whether you need a primary visual or an extensive array of designs, we've got you covered.
- **Transparent Pricing**: I believe in honesty and openness, especially in Pricing. That's why we've revised our pricing structure to be as transparent and straightforward as possible.
- **Valuing Our Art**: Every piece of our department's artwork results from hard work and creative talent. This year, we ensure this effort is rightly valued and billed.

## **Educating and Engaging with Our Customers:**

My mission is to help our clients understand the importance of good design in brand storytelling. In 2024, I'm committed to furthering these educational efforts, ensuring our clients know the value of what they're getting.

## **Our Marketing Strategy for Growth:**

We're also introducing a marketing fund initiative, allowing our sales team to use allocated art hours for client engagement. This approach is about showing, not just telling, the potential of what we can create together.

## **My Promise to You:**

As we enter this new year, I promise to keep pushing the boundaries of what's possible in apparel design. We take every step with precision and care, from the first sketch to the final product. We use your vision as our own and create something extraordinary together.
