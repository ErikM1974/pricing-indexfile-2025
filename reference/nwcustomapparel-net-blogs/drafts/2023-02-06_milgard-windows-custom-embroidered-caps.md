## Different Methods For Adding Your Logo

So you're looking to get custom embroidered caps with your logo on 'em. You want something that stands out, and I can tell you this - it's not gonna be just any old design! That's why I'm here to show you the different methods for adding your logo. It's time to go over all the options so you know what'll work best for you and make sure everyone knows who they're dealing with.

First up is direct [embroidery](https://nwcustomapparel.net/services/custom-embroidery/). This is where the design or logo gets stitched directly onto the cap. There are tons of variations in terms of thread types, stitch density, fabric weight, size of graphics, etc., but no matter what combination of those factors you pick, trust me when I say it looks great! Your design will have maximum impact when choosing this method, plus it’s one of the most cost-effective solutions too – everybody wins!

Next, we have digitizing services that create a ‘template’ from your [artwork](https://nwcustomapparel.net/services/artwork/) or logo file by converting it into a format readable by an embroidery machine. The template then gets sewn directly onto the garment using colored threads as per your specifications. Digitizing allows for more detailed designs in comparison to direct embroidery and gives greater control over color and placement accuracy within small areas – perfect if precision matters to ya!

Now that we've gone through all these methods, you should have a better idea about how to add your logo to those custom caps. So don't wait around – take action now and let everyone see exactly who they're dealing with!
