## Understanding Under Armour's Customization Process

It’s important to know that Under Armour has very specific rules about how their products are customized to maintain their brand integrity and quality. This is crucial information for any order.

### Under Armour's Embroidery Policy

Unlike many other brands, Under Armour *does not allow* its distributors (like NW Custom Apparel) or any external embellishers to directly decorate their goods. All **Embroidered Under Armour Apparel** must be decorated by an Under Armour-approved embellisher, which is typically facilitated through their primary wholesale supplier, Alphabroder. This embroidery is often done at their distribution center in Kansas City.

### Why This Policy Exists

This strict policy is in place to ensure absolute brand integrity and consistent quality. Under Armour wants to guarantee that any logo applied to their premium apparel meets their high standards for craftsmanship and durability. It helps protect the brand's reputation and ensures that your custom logo looks as professional as the garment itself.

We manage the communication, design approval, and logistics, ensuring your order adheres to all of Under Armour's requirements. ## Important Under Armour Brand Guidelines & Restrictions

Under Armour protects its brand carefully, and there are strict guidelines for product use and decoration. NW Custom Apparel ensures all orders comply with these rules.

- **Prohibited Channels:** Under Armour products are **not** to be sold into retail stores, discount stores, warehouse clubs, online retailers (like Amazon or eBay), or other promotional products distributors intending to re-sell the products into these channels. These are strictly for corporate end-users.
- **Corporate Customer Identity:** You must provide the identity of the corporate customer, the purpose of how the Under Armour brand will be used (e.g., employee uniforms, corporate gifts), and the specific logo application that will be applied to all products.
- **Mandatory Decoration:** All Under Armour products *must* be decorated with the corporate client’s logo prior to delivery to the end-user. Un-decorated Under Armour blanks are not permitted for distribution.
- **Prohibited Decorations:** Decorations relating to colleges, universities, collegiate sports teams, golf courses, golf clubs, or golf country clubs are expressly prohibited. This ensures the corporate line is distinct from their retail/athletic team lines.

These rules ensure the integrity and premium positioning of the Under Armour brand in the corporate and custom apparel market.
