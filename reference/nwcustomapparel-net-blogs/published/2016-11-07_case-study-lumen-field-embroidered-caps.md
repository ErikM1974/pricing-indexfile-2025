# Case Study: 300 Custom Caps for CenturyLink Field

## The Challenge: A High-Stakes Rush Order of football stadium caps

On a Friday afternoon, we received an urgent call from the team at **Lumen Field** (formerly CenturyLink Field). They had a last-minute need for **300 custom embroidered caps** for an upcoming Monday Night Football game between the Seahawks and the Bills.

The deadline was non-negotiable: the caps had to be completed and delivered by 1 PM on Monday, just in time for the game. This was a high-volume order with an extremely tight turnaround, demanding not only speed but also flawless quality to represent one of the nation's most iconic stadiums.

## The Northwest Solution: Speed and Precision

At Northwest Custom Apparel, we pride ourselves on delivering when others can't. We immediately fast-tracked the project, demonstrating our ability to handle complex, large-scale rush orders.

Our team swiftly processed the artwork for direct embroidery. With a clear focus and unwavering efficiency, our expert embroiderers got to work, utilizing our high-speed, multi-head machines. We leveraged our deep expertise and dedicated equipment to ensure every single one of the 300 caps met our high standards for quality and detail, all while operating on an exceptionally tight schedule.
