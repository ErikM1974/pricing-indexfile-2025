# How NW Custom Apparel Helped Cornerstone Electric Elevate Their Workwear

## Case Study Of Service T-Shirts

For nearly four decades, **Cornerstone Electric** has been a trusted name in home services across the **Seattle metro area**. Their crew is out in the field daily, fixing electrical problems, handling installs, and making life easier for homeowners. But for a long time, their workwear didn’t match the professionalism they brought to every job.

Their old supplier sent them **uniform shirts** that didn’t last. The fabric wore out fast. The stitching wasn’t strong. And the customer service? Just plain frustrating. Orders got delayed. Sizes were wrong. Shirts looked shabby in just a few weeks. It wasn’t working.
