## History of Cutter and Buck

In 1989, Cutter and Buck commenced their business with the help of lead designer Joey Rodolfo. He gained popularity by designing embroidered golf shirts in vibrant colors, explicitly catering to golfers. These shirts became a massive success among pro shops throughout the country. Later on, Joey joined Tommy Bahama as their chief designer. Northwest Custom Apparel now features the [**Tommy Bahama**](https://nwcustomapparel.net/custom-embroidered-tommy-bahama/) shirt line.
