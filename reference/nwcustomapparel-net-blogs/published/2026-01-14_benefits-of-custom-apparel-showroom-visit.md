# Why a Showroom Visit Guarantees Better Custom Apparel

Many business owners order custom branded gear online and end up disappointed when the box arrives. The navy blue looks purple, the heavyweight hoodie feels thin, or the sizing runs small. At Northwest Custom Apparel, we believe that high-quality branding requires a tactile experience.

Our showroom in Milton, Washington, serves as a resource for clients who want to eliminate guesswork. After 27 years in production, I have found that ten minutes of feeling fabric samples saves hours of frustration later.

## The Tangible Advantage: Touch, Feel, and Fit

Digital photos cannot convey the "hand feel" of a garment. A 6-ounce cotton t-shirt feels significantly different from a 4-ounce ringspun blend, yet they look identical on a smartphone screen.

When you visit our showroom, you can compare different fabric weights side-by-side. This is especially important for our [custom embroidered sweatshirts](https://nwcustomapparel.net/services/custom-embroidered-sweatshirts-hoodies/). You can feel the interior fleece lining and test the stretch of the cuffs. Fit testing is another major benefit. We maintain size runs of our most popular brands, including Port Authority and Carhartt, so your team gets the right fit the first time.

## Color Accuracy and Thread Matching

Computer monitors vary in color calibration. A "Forest Green" on your laptop might look like "Olive" on ours. For businesses with strict brand identity guidelines, this discrepancy is a problem.

In our showroom, we use physical Madeira thread charts and Pantone matching systems. We hold actual thread cones against your printed brand assets to find a perfect match. This ensures that the logo on your [custom screen printed hoodies](https://nwcustomapparel.net/services/screen-printing/custom-hoodies/) matches the logo on your [embroidered hats](https://nwcustomapparel.net/services/custom-embroidery-caps/) exactly.

## What to Expect During Your Showroom Consultation

We keep our showroom organized so you can make decisions quickly. When you arrive, we guide you through a three-step process:

1. **The Fabric Review:** We pull samples based on your environment (construction, office, or event).
2. **The "Scrap Library" Check:** We show you actual embroidery samples on similar fabrics to demonstrate stitch clarity.
3. **The Project Quote:** We finalize your garment selection and provide an all-inclusive price.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Do I need an appointment to visit the showroom?**

We welcome walk-ins during our standard business hours. However, scheduling a brief appointment ensures that a production expert like myself is available to discuss the technical details of your logo and fabric choices.

**Can I take samples home to show my team?**

We offer a sample program for established clients and large projects. You can check out specific garments to verify sizing and style with your staff before committing to a bulk order.

**Is the showroom pricing different from your website?**

The pricing remains consistent. The showroom simply allows you to confirm your selections before we start the production process. We find this prevents the hidden costs of re-ordering due to sizing or color errors.

**Where is the showroom located?**

Yes. We have dedicated sections for screen printing, embroidery, and heat transfer. You can compare how your logo looks across different decoration methods to decide which fits your budget and style.
