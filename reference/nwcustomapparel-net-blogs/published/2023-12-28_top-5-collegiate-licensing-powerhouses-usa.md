# Top 5 Collegiate Licensing Powerhouses in the USA

In the ever-evolving world of collegiate licensing and NIL deals with college athletes, choosing the right partner for your needs can be daunting. I have been in apparel licensing since 1997 when I worked with Collegiate Licensing and Licensing Resources as a licensee with [Northwest Custom Apparel](https://nwcustomapparel.net/). I aim to simplify the choice by rating the top five collegiate licensing companies in the United States. I have evaluated these companies based on their market influence, product diversity, quality, and unique selling points. Each company is rated on a scale of 1 to 5, with 5 being the highest, to help you understand their strengths and weaknesses in the collegiate licensing market.

## Top Collegiate Licensing Companies USA

### '47 Brand (4/5)

**Why:** Esteemed for quality headwear and apparel with strong sports league partnerships, though slightly less diverse in collegiate-specific products.

#### A Legacy in Sports Lifestyle Branding

Under Ms. Kathleen Crane's guidance, the company has established a strong partnership with major sports leagues and offers an extensive range of products that resonate with sports enthusiasts.

## Conclusion

My journey through the top 5 collegiate licensing suppliers has highlighted diverse specialties, from '47 Brand's sports lifestyle branding to CI Sports' extensive collegiate apparel. College Concepts Sport leads the pack with its exceptional sleepwear line, while Northwest Custom Apparel stands out for its cutting-edge direct-to-garment printing technology. All-Star Dogs brings a unique offering with its collegiate-themed pet apparel. Each of these companies showcases distinct strengths, making them leaders in their respective niches. Congratulations to all for their remarkable contributions to the collegiate licensing industry, innovatively enhancing college spirit.
