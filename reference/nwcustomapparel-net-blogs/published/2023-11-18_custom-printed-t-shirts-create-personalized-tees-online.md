## Why Brian, the Lead Operator at NW Custom Apparel, Loves DTG Printing

In the world of [custom apparel](https://nwcustomapparel.net/cause-marketing-custom-apparel-case-study/), there are many ways to get your designs and art onto a garment. Screen printing Seattle is an old solid with crisp but limited order sizes and colors. Vinyl transfers are great but have a feel to them that might not be the best for every situation. My favorite is DTG printing for many reasons, but as an operator and graphic designer, I’ll review a few that sold me on DTG.

Some edits are needed to get the perfect print. The process doesn’t require color separations or screen burning, unlike [screen print](https://nwcustomapparel.net/rush-t-shirts/). It’s plug-and-play, allowing artists and designers to quickly get whatever they have dreamed of onto a t-shirt.

DTG machines are excellent for small runs of 1 or 2 shirts. As a result, Amazon uses these machines to [print thousands of single shirts](https://nwcustomapparel.net/direct-to-garment/).

### It's Environmentally Friendly

The ink and pre-treatment for DTG machines are mainly water-based, making them have a low impact on the [environment](https://nwcustomapparel.net/safe-ink-kids-tees/). Another process may use plastics or other chemicals to help the ink adhere to the shirt, or the ink itself may harm the environment.

## This is How a DTG Printer Works

The Kornit printers we use are the best for DTG. They create an outstanding print in a short amount of time. Our excellent staff and the techy machines produce a great shirt. A computer attaches to the printer to load files and apply settings. However, this only scratches the surface of the machine's operation.

A pneumatic vacuum system powered by a compressor moves the ink through the machine and onto the shirt. It also sprays the t-shirt inside the printer for a simple pre-treatment process. The pallets and rails use magnets to glide back and forth during printing. The built-in heaters inside the print orifices keep them at the right temperature.

[dgt\_promo\_section]

A humidifier inside the machine ensures the ink is moist and doesn't dry up before hitting the shirt. In addition, there are plenty of pumps, filters, and tubes. These create a complex unison of tech that allows the machine to do its job. More importantly, it gives the customer the shirt they want.

### Maintaining Custom-Printed T-Shirts: Care Tips for Vibrant Prints

To properly care for your custom-printed t-shirts and ensure the print remains vibrant while maintaining the shirt's quality over time, follow these simple steps:

- Always turn your t-shirt inside out before washing it to protect the print.
- Use cold water and mild detergent, avoiding bleach or fabric softeners.
- Opt for a gentle wash cycle or hand wash for delicate prints.
- After washing, hang your shirt to air dry or use low heat in the dryer.
- Avoid ironing directly on the print; place a cloth between the iron and the design if necessary.

Following these guidelines keeps your custom-printed tees looking fresh and lasting longer!
