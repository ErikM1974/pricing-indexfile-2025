## White Coffee Mug is the Best

The easiest color to print on is a white coffee mug. It is cheaper because you don't have to print white under-base which is an extra print color. The color of your logo will pop having the white substrate underneath it.

## How Much Do They Weigh

A box of 144 coffee mugs is around 45 lbs, which can be expensive to ship. The factory is located in Los Angeles and they will give freight breaks if you are shipping to Seattle. The mugs need to be properly packaged before shipping to decrease the possibility of a broken mug.

## Who uses White Coffee Mugs?

These mugs were originally used at the Seattle Starbucks down at the Pike Place Market back in 1971. Since then the white c-handle mugs have become very popular as a promotional item for businesses, restaurants, and schools in or near the city of Seattle.
