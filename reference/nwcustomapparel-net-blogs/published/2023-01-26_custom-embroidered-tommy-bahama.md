The critical first step is [embroidery digitization](https://nwcustomapparel.net/logo-digitizing-for-embroidery/). For luxury apparel, this process requires technical knowledge to prevent distortion:

## Maintaining the Quality of Your Embroidered Tommy Bahama Shirts

![caring for your tommy bahama](https://nwcustomapparel.net/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/DSC02260-1024x683.webp)

Take good care of your Tommy Bahama shirt.

To ensure your investment maintains its pristine look and the vibrancy of your embroidered logo for years, proper care is essential:

- **Turn Inside Out:** Always turn the garment inside out before washing to protect the embroidery from abrasion.
- **Cold Water & Mild Detergent:** Wash gently using cold water. Avoid harsh chemicals like bleach or fabric softeners.
- **Air Dry or Low Heat:** Air drying is highly recommended. If using a dryer, opt for a low heat setting.
- **Iron with Care:** Always place a thin cloth over the embroidered area. **Never iron directly on the embroidery** .
