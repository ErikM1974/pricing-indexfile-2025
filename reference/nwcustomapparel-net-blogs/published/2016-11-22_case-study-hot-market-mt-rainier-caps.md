Handling high-stakes, time-sensitive projects is our specialty. We thrive when deadlines are tight and quality cannot be compromised. This commitment to performance is best proven by our long-standing work with **International Mountain Guides (IMG)**, a global leader in mountaineering expeditions.

This project specifically highlights the monumental achievement of guide George Dunn's **500th summit of Mt. Rainier**. An accomplishment of this magnitude required immediate, custom recognition. This case study serves as direct proof of our consistent quality and **Hot Market Production** capacity, a relationship we have proudly maintained since before 1999.

## The Commemorative Project: Precision and Prestige

This was not a standard corporate order; it was a tribute to history. [George Dunn](http://www.mountainguides.com/dunn.shtml)'s 500th summit required a product worthy of the achievement.

### Designing Custom Commemorative Caps for Mountaineering Legends

![emblem](https://nwcustomapparel.net/wp-content/uploads/2016/11/emblem-1-300x144-1.webp)

We created **Custom Commemorative Caps** for George Dunn and his fellow legendary guide, [Brent Okita](http://www.theolympian.com/outdoors/article82391792.html), the only two climbers to reach the 500-summit mark. The caps feature the iconic **International Mountain Guide Logo**. High-quality **Embroidery** on the caps was non-negotiable, ensuring durability and clear branding in harsh, high-altitude environments.

### The Details: Durable Emblems for Trade

The project also involved creating specialized **commemorative emblems** (patches). Climbers frequently trade these emblems or wear them on the outside of technical jackets. This specific use case demands maximum durability and high-thread count precision, attributes we guarantee in every patch we produce.
