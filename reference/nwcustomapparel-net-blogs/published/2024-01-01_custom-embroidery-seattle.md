## What’s the difference between direct embroidery and embroidered patches?

Both options have unique benefits depending on your needs:

✔ Direct Embroidery – This method stitches the design directly onto fabric (shirts, hats, uniforms). It’s long-lasting, professional-looking, and won’t peel or fade like printed designs.  
✔ Embroidered Patches – **[Patches are stitched](/services/custom-patch/)** separately and then applied to garments. These are great for high-detail logos, removable branding, or if you want to reuse them on different items.

Need help deciding? Our embroidery specialists can recommend the best option based on your fabric and usage.

### Can I wash and dry embroidered apparel like regular clothing?

Yes, but to maintain embroidery quality, follow these care tips:

Washing: Use cold water on a gentle cycle. Avoid bleach and harsh detergents.  
Drying: Air dry if possible. If using a dryer, low heat only to prevent shrinking.  
Ironing: Avoid direct ironing on the embroidery. Use a cloth over it if needed.  
Storage: Keep embroidered garments flat or hung up to prevent wrinkles.

Following these steps will ensure your custom embroidery lasts for years without fading or fraying.
