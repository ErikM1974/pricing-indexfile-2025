## Why Embroidery Wins for School Uniforms

### Durability That Outlasts the School Year

Embroidery weaves thread into fabric structure. Screen printing lays ink on top. The difference shows after repeated washing.

Independent testing demonstrates embroidered designs maintain 99.2% stitch retention after 250 wash cycles . Screen printing fades at 20–30 washes; heat transfer vinyl peels at 15–25 washes. For 195 school days annually, embroidery eliminates mid-year replacement burden.

**When embroidery is wrong:** Single-event use, 2,000+ units with 1-year replacement cycles, or extreme budget constraints where initial cost outweighs durability value.

## What Makes a Quality School Uniform Polo

### Fabric Selection

**Piqué cotton (180–220 GSM):** The standard. Honeycomb texture supports embroidery without distortion. Breathable for Pacific Northwest climate. Professional appearance maintains crispness through school day.

**Jersey knit** is softer, and more stretch. It's preferred for sensory-sensitive students and requires additional embroidery backing.

**Performance blends:** Polyester/cotton with moisture-wicking. 15–20% cost premium. Ideal for active students or physical education programs.

### Sizing That Includes Every Student

Comprehensive programs accommodate diverse body types:

- **Regular (XS–XL):** 65% of students
- **Husky/Plus:** 20% of students—extended waist, roomier cut
- **Slim:** 10% of students—narrower waist, tapered fit
- **Tall:** 5% of students—+2 inch body length

Washington State data: 32% of students require extended sizing options . Programs failing to accommodate this population violate equity requirements.

### Embroidery Specifications That Last

Northwest Custom Apparel production standards:

- **Left chest placement:** 3.5–4.5 inches wide, 7–9 inches down from shoulder
- **Thread type:** Polyester (colorfast, bleach-resistant) for daily wear
- **Stitch density:** 6,000–8,000 stitches—sufficient coverage without rigidity
- **Minimum text:** 4mm height for wash durability

**Quality verification:** Digital proof approval, first-article inspection, 10% random sampling during production.
