# Case Study: How Arrow Lumber Grew Its Brand with Northwest Custom Apparel's Digital Printing & Embroidery Services

In a competitive market, a brand's visibility is its most valuable asset. For Arrow Lumber, a leading name in the building and construction industry, the challenge was to cut through the noise and connect directly with their customers: contractors and tradespeople. They knew traditional advertising methods weren't working. Their solution? A strategic partnership with Northwest Custom Apparel to turn their customers into "walking billboards" with [high-quality branded apparel](https://nwcustomapparel.net/benefits-of-branded-workwear-for-businesses/). This is a story of smart marketing, a strong partnership, and the tangible power of a well-placed logo.

## The Problem: Reaching Contractors in a Digital-Fatigued World

Arrow Lumber, like many businesses selling to the trade, faced a unique marketing challenge. Their customers are not typical office workers but contractors and on-site workers. They spend their days outdoors, on job sites, and in workshops—not scrolling through email campaigns or clicking banner ads. Traditional advertising, whether digital or print, rarely reached them where it mattered.

Arrow Lumber needed a solution that was visible, practical, and consistent. They needed a way to ensure their brand was present wherever their customers worked and gathered.

## The Solution: Making Customers Your Most Effective Marketers

At Northwest Custom Apparel, we collaborated with Arrow Lumber to develop a simple yet profoundly effective marketing strategy. We shifted their budget from fleeting digital ads to durable, high-impact promotional products. By providing their customers and teams with top-quality, branded apparel like [digitally printed t-shirts](https://nwcustomapparel.net/direct-to-garment/) and embroidered caps, Arrow Lumber could turn every job site, meeting, and daily errand into a powerful branding opportunity.

![A printed t-shirt and embroidered cap with the Arrow Lumber logo](https://nwcustomapparel.net/wp-content/uploads/2016/04/arrow-lumber-branded-apparel.webp)

This approach is rooted in a fundamental truth: people love merchandise they can wear. And when it’s comfortable and high-quality, they become your most loyal brand advocates.

## The Results: Quantifying the Value of Branded Apparel

The tangible results of this strategy are a testament to its effectiveness. A study in the promotional products industry shows a clear return on investment.

- **Custom T-Shirts:** At a cost of approximately **$8.00 per shirt** for digital printing, a single shirt worn on a job site can be seen by at least **50 people per week**. Over a year, that’s over **2,600 impressions**. This translates to a cost per impression of less than **1/3 of a cent**, making it one of the most affordable and effective marketing channels available.
- **[Embroidered Caps](https://nwcustomapparel.net/our-caps/):** Studies show that a high-quality, embroidered cap can generate over **3,000 impressions** before it’s replaced. At an average cost of **$10.00**, this results in a remarkable cost per impression of less than **1/3 of a cent**. A cap not only promotes a brand but also builds team morale and a sense of shared identity.

## A Partnership Built on Results and Relationships

The collaboration between Arrow Lumber and Northwest Custom Apparel goes beyond transactions. **Barney Wagner**, CEO and Founder of Arrow Lumber, along with his colleague **Sean**, have become true partners in the process. When they walk into the shop to reorder caps and tees because demand is high and the shelves are empty, it’s proof that the strategy is working.

This partnership demonstrates how a clear vision, the right promotional products, and strong collaboration can turn marketing dollars into lasting visibility and customer loyalty.

Arrow Lumber’s success highlights a simple lesson: in industries where personal connection and visibility matter, branded apparel is one of the most effective marketing tools available. By partnering with [Northwest Custom Apparel](https://nwcustomapparel.net/about/), they transformed ordinary clothing into a powerful, low-cost advertising channel—one that continues to deliver results every day, on every job site.
