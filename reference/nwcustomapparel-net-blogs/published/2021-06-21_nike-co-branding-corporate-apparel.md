Nike shirts are made from **patented performance material** exclusive to Nike only. This material, such as **Dri-FIT**, demands specialized decoration expertise that typical embroiderers lack.

Nike's performance fabrics are lightweight and designed to wick moisture away. Standard embroidery techniques, which involve dense stitching and heavy backing, damage these fabrics. Incorrect technique causes **puckering**, thread breakage, and destroys the wicking functionality, compromising the very reason you chose Nike.
