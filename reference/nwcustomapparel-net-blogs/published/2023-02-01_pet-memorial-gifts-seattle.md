## What We Can Print for Your Pet Memorial

For Seattle's cold months, a memorial hoodie carries more weight than a T-shirt. We print pet portraits on midweight fleece hoodies using the same DTG process. The print sits soft on the fabric — not a stiff transfer that cracks in the wash.

## How to Order a Custom Pet Memorial Shirt

### Send Us Your Photo

A high-resolution image works best. Smartphone photos are fine if they are clear and well-lit. We need at least 300 DPI for a sharp print. If the photo is blurry, dark, or cropped too tight, we will tell you before we print. We do not guess. We do not upsample low-quality images and hope for the best.

### Choose Your Garment

Most pet memorial orders are printed on 100% cotton or cotton-polyester blend T-shirts. Cotton accepts DTG ink cleanly and produces the softest hand feel. For hoodies, we recommend midweight fleece. See our [cotton vs polyester guide](https://nwcustomapparel.net/cotton-vs-polyester-guide/) if you are unsure which fabric fits your use case.

### Approve the Proof

We send a digital proof within 24 hours. You approve it. We print it. No surprises. If you need the shirt for a specific date — a memorial service, an anniversary, a birthday — tell us when you order. We offer rush turnaround when our schedule allows.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Can you print any pet photo?**

Yes, as long as the image resolution is sufficient. We print portraits, action shots, and posed photos. Black-and-white images work well too. We do not recommend group photos where the pet is small in the frame — the detail gets lost when scaled to a shirt.

**What if I only want text — no photo?**

We can do that. Names, dates, quotes, and paw prints all work on DTG and screen print. Embroidery is also an option for text-only designs on hoodies or polo shirts.

**Will the photo fade in the wash?**

DTG prints last 50+ washes with proper care. Wash cold, tumble dry low, turn inside out. The ink bonds with cotton fibers. It does not sit on top like an iron-on transfer. For a garment you plan to wear frequently, embroidery is the more durable option.

**Can you print on both sides of the shirt?**

Yes. Front and back printing is standard. A common layout is the pet portrait on the front and a name with dates on the back. We price this as a two-location print.

**Do you also print memorial shirts for human funerals?**

Yes. We produce [custom funeral t-shirts and RIP shirts](https://nwcustomapparel.net/direct-to-garment/memorial-t-shirts/) for memorial services, family reunions, and anniversary remembrances. The process is identical to our pet memorial shirts — DTG photo printing, in-house production, 48-hour turnaround on single pieces. Many families who order pet memorials from us later order human memorial apparel, or they refer others who need the same service.

**Do you offer rush orders?**

Yes, when our production schedule allows. Rush DTG orders can ship same day. Rush screen print orders require 5 business days minimum. Call us at 253-922-5793 to check availability.
