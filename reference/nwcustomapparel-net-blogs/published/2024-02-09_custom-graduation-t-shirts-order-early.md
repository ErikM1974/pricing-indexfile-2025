## What sizes should I order for High School Graduation Shirts?

Considering the diverse body sizes among graduates, ordering various sizes for your high school graduation shirts is essential. You'll want to ensure every graduating student feels comfortable and included in this memorable celebration. Surveying or gathering data on students' preferred t-shirt sizes is a good idea. This approach can help you stay within specific sizes while running short on others. Remember to start this survey 3-4 months before graduation. If you are a high school in Washington State, March is a great month to start the survey and get the student's feedback.

Typically, it's wise to order a broad range of sizes, from small to XL, considering that some students may prefer a looser fit and have gained weight in the last 3 months. Remember, plus sizes also; ensuring a few 2XL and 3XL options can be a thoughtful consideration. These percentages are just a starting point; you should adjust them based on your student body's specific needs and preferences.
