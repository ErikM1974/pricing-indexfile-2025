## FAQ: Custom Embroidered Jackets for Businesses

**What materials are best for personalized embroidered jackets?**

The best materials depend on your specific needs. For outdoor work, consider water-resistant or insulated fabrics. Lightweight materials like polyester blends are great for active environments, while cotton jackets offer breathability for casual wear. Always choose high-quality materials that can withstand embroidery without tearing.

**How long does it take to produce custom embroidered jackets?**

Production times vary based on order size and design complexity. On average, smaller orders can take 7-10 business days, while larger or highly detailed projects might take 2-3 weeks. Be sure to check with your embroidery provider for exact timelines.

**Are there limits to the design size or detail for embroidery?**

Most embroidery machines can handle intricate designs, but there are practical limits to size and detail. Large, highly detailed logos may require adjustments to ensure clarity. Discuss your design with the provider to find the best solution for your branding.

**Can I order a small batch of custom jackets?**

Yes! Many providers offer flexible options, including no minimum order quantities. This is perfect for small businesses or testing a new design before committing to a larger batch.

**How do I maintain and clean embroidered jackets?**

To prolong the life of your jackets, follow care instructions closely. Most embroidered jackets can be machine-washed on a gentle cycle with mild detergent. Avoid using bleach or high heat when drying, as this can damage the embroidery.
