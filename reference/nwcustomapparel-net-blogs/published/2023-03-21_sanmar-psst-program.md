## What is SanMar PSST

[SanMar's](https://www.sanmar.com/) PSST program, an innovative logistics solution, streamlines your ordering and decoration process by ensuring your products arrive pre-sorted and ready for immediate customization.

SanMar's PSST program is your ultimate solution to the inventory sorting and counting hassle. We guarantee that your orders will be 100% complete, saving you time and effort in handling them. With PSST, you can jump into production and enjoy a seamless workflow. Join us and experience the convenience and efficiency that PSST has to offer!

This program isn't just for bulk orders; even single-item orders ship free. PPDS, or Packed Separately Shipped Together, epitomizes efficiency, providing you and your customers seamless service.
