# Custom Samoan Funeral T-Shirts and Memorial Apparel in Seattle

## We Print Memorial Apparel for Samoan Families in Seattle

Samoan funerals (fa'alavelave) bring together extended family, church communities, and village connections. It is common for 100 to 300 people to attend. Many families wear matching memorial shirts to honor the deceased and show family unity.

Northwest Custom Apparel produces custom funeral t-shirts for Samoan and Pacific Islander families across the Seattle-Tacoma area in Milton, Washington. We have printed memorial apparel for families in Kent, Federal Way, Tacoma, and Seattle. We understand that these shirts are not merchandise. They are part of the funeral tradition.

We print every order in our own facility. We do not outsource to a third party, and not dropship from another state. You can call us, visit our shop, or email us directly. We speak with the same person who runs the printer.

## What Samoan Funeral T-Shirts Look Like

### Photo Memorial Shirts

The most common request we receive is a photo of the deceased printed on a black or white shirt, with text in both English and Samoan. Our Kornit Storm Hexa DTG printer handles full-color photographs with no color limit. We can print a portrait with the same clarity as a photograph.

![fa'alavelave t-shirts](https://nwcustomapparel.net/wp-content/uploads/2023/03/samoa-2-1300x731.webp)

Typical layout: photo on the front, name and dates on the back, family motto or scripture reference on the sleeve. We print these on 100% cotton or cotton-polyester blend shirts for softness during long funeral services.

### Cultural Motif Designs

Some families prefer traditional symbols over photographs. We have printed shirts with:

- **Manumea** (tooth-billed pigeon, national bird of Samoa)
- **Tatau patterns** (traditional tattoo motifs representing family lineage)
- **Fale** (house) and **vaka** (canoe) symbols
- **Ie toga** (fine mat) geometric patterns

These designs require high-resolution artwork. If your family has a specific pattern or crest, send us the file. We can adapt it for print. If you need design assistance, we work with a local Samoan graphic artist who understands the cultural significance of each symbol.

### Bulk Orders for Large Families

Samoan funerals often require 50 to 200 shirts. For orders over 24 pieces, [screen printing](https://nwcustomapparel.net/services/screen-printing/) is more cost-effective than DTG. Screen printing produces bold, opaque color on dark garments. It is ideal for text-heavy designs and single-color motifs on black shirts.

Turnaround for bulk screen print orders is 10 business days. For rush orders, we offer 5-day production when our schedule allows.

## Fabric and Color Choices for Samoan Memorials

### Traditional Colors

Black and white are the standard colors for Samoan funeral shirts. Some families add red or purple as accent colors. We stock Gildan and Independent Trading Co. blanks in black and white in all sizes (XS to 5XL). Extended sizes are common for these orders.

### Fabric Weight for All-Day Wear

Funeral services in the Samoan tradition can last all day. The shirt must be comfortable for long wear.

| Weight | Best For | Our Recommendation |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Lightweight (180–220 GSM) | Indoor services, summer funerals | Acceptable for short events |
| Midweight (240–280 GSM) | Most funerals, year-round wear | Our standard recommendation |
| Heavyweight (300–400 GSM) | Outdoor vigils, winter services | Available on request |

We recommend midweight cotton or cotton-polyester blend for most orders. It holds print well, breathes during long services, and does not shrink excessively in the wash. For families who want hoodies instead of t-shirts, we offer [custom embroidered sweatshirts](https://nwcustomapparel.net/services/custom-embroidered-sweatshirts-hoodies/) with stitched names and dates.

## How to Order Samoan Funeral T-Shirts

### Send Us Your Design or Photo

A high-resolution image works best. For photo memorials, we need at least 300 DPI. For cultural motifs, vector files (AI, EPS, PDF) produce the sharpest print. We review every file before production and flag issues. We do not print low-resolution images and hope for the best.

### Choose Your Garment Style

Most families order unisex crew neck t-shirts in black. We also offer:

- V-neck shirts (popular with younger family members)
- Long-sleeve shirts (for winter funerals)
- Hoodies (for outdoor vigils and cemetery services)
- Youth sizes (for children attending the funeral)
