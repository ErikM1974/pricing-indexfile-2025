Important DTG Printing Information

  • ⚠️ No Gildan Products: We cannot print on Gildan brand apparel due to fabric treatment that prevents proper ink adhesion.
  • ✅ Best Results: We prefer 100% cotton garments for optimal print quality and durability.
  • 💡 Pro Tip: Dark colors like black, navy, and dark heather grey provide the best contrast for vibrant DTG prints.
