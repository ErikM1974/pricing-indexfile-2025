Customer-Supplied Pricing Info Click to expand
Customer-Supplied Pricing: Customer brings their OWN garments/caps for embroidery. Higher price because we have no garment margin and assume more risk.
Why is this 4x wholesale pricing?
  • No garment margin cushion - embroidery must carry full profit
  • Higher defect risk - can't replace customer's items
  • Quality inspection overhead - customer blanks vary widely
  • We don't seek this work, but price to profit when we do it
