Who Needs a Webstore?

Companies experiencing these challenges benefit most from a webstore:

  • Team members spending excessive time on apparel requests
  • Difficulty tracking available supplies
  • Inconsistent inventory management
  • Complex employee uniform programs
  • Challenges with exchanges and returns
  • Need for streamlined new hire onboarding

Best Practices

  • Start with 4-6 different garment styles
  • Limit colors to 1-3 per style for simplicity
  • Keep logo placement consistent across items
  • Begin with core items (polos, t-shirts, jackets)
  • Consider seasonal needs upfront
  • Designate a single point of contact for orders
