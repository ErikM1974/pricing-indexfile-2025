Store types & pricing Open/Close vs On-Demand

Two store models

Pick the one that matches your customer's ordering pattern
Open/Close
+$2.00 per item sold
Seasonal or campaign-driven. Staff opens the store for a defined window (1–6 weeks typical), collects orders, closes it, runs the production batch. Best for: team fundraisers, back-to-school drives, new-hire onboarding, one-time gear orders.
On-Demand
+$6.00 per item sold
Year-round, always open. Customer controls open/close. Each order ships individually. Best for: ongoing uniform programs, customer-service give-aways, employee perks, anytime ordering convenience.

Best practices & track record How to set up for success

Setup best practices

• Start with 4–6 different garment styles — too many overwhelms supporters
• Limit colors to 1–3 per style for simplicity (and faster production)
• Keep logo placement consistent across items
• Begin with core items — polos, t-shirts, jackets — before specialty
• Plan for seasonal needs upfront (fall jackets, summer tees)
• Designate a single point of contact at the customer for orders/questions
