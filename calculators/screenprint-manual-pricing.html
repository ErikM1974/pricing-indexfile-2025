Manual Screen Print Pricing Guide

Calculate screen print pricing with just a calculator, pen, and paper

Step-by-Step Formula

STEP 1: Calculate Garment Cost with Margin
   Garment Total = Blank Cost ÷ Margin Denominator

STEP 2: Calculate Screen Print Costs
   Print Cost = (Base Cost + Flash Charge) × Total Colors × Locations

STEP 3: Add Setup Fees
   Setup Total = $30 × Total Colors × Locations

STEP 4: Apply Special Charges
   Safety Stripe Surcharge = $2.00 × Stripe Locations

STEP 5: Add LTM Fee (if quantity < 48)
   LTM Fee = $50.00 (flat fee)

STEP 6: Apply Half-Dollar Ceiling
   Final Price = CEILING(Total, $0.50)

Worked Examples
Example 1: Basic Order (24 pieces, 1 color, front only)
Order: 24 pieces @ $4.00 blank cost
1. Margin: $4.00 ÷ 0.45 = $8.89 each
2. Print: ($2.45 + $0.35) × 1 color × 1 location = $2.80
3. Setup: $30 × 1 color × 1 location = $30.00
4. Subtotal: 24 × $8.89 + $2.80 + $30.00 = $246.16
5. No LTM (≥24 pieces), No safety stripes
6. Round up: $246.16 → $246.50
Example 2: LTM Order with Safety Stripes (12 pieces, 2 colors, front + back)
Order: 12 pieces @ $3.50 blank, front (2 colors + safety) + back (1 color)
1. Margin: $3.50 ÷ 0.45 = $7.78 each
2. Safety adds +1 color: Front = 3 colors, Back = 1 color
3. Print: ($2.45 + $0.35) × 3 × 1 + ($2.45 + $0.00) × 1 × 1 = $10.85
4. Setup: $30 × (3 + 1) = $120.00
5. Safety surcharge: $2.00 × 1 location = $2.00
6. Subtotal: 12 × $7.78 + $10.85 + $120.00 + $2.00 = $226.21
7. LTM Fee: +$50.00 (< 48 pieces)
8. Round up: $276.21 → $276.50
Example 3: Large Order (200 pieces, 3 colors, front only)
Order: 200 pieces @ $5.25 blank cost
1. Margin: $5.25 ÷ 0.55 = $9.55 each
2. Print: ($1.45 + $0.35) × 3 colors × 1 location = $5.40
3. Setup: $30 × 3 colors × 1 location = $90.00
4. Subtotal: 200 × $9.55 + $5.40 + $90.00 = $2005.40
5. No LTM (≥48 pieces), No safety stripes
6. Round up: $2005.40 → $2005.50
