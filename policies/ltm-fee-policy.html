Essential guide for orders under 24 pieces

Purpose

The Less Than Minimum (LTM) fee ensures we recover administrative costs associated with small orders while maintaining transparency and fairness. This $50 fee applies to orders under 24 pieces and covers:

  • CSR Coordination: Order entry, quoting, and customer communication
  • Purchasing: Sourcing blanks from suppliers like SanMar
  • Receiving: Unboxing, counting, and quality checks

Important: The LTM fee does NOT include production setup, design, or digitizing costs. These are billed separately if applicable.

How the Fee Works

The $50 fee is prorated across all items in the order and added to the 24-piece base price:

Example Calculation:

10 embroidered hats

  • Base price (24-piece rate): $15.00 per hat
  • LTM fee per item: $50 ÷ 10 = $5.00
  • Final price per hat: $20.00
Another Example:

5 DTG shirts

  • Base price (24-piece rate): $12.00 per shirt
  • LTM fee per item: $50 ÷ 5 = $10.00
  • Final price per shirt: $22.00

Step-by-Step Process

  1. Check Order Quantity: Is it under 24 pieces? If yes, continue.
  2. Identify Service Type: Embroidery, DTG, DTF, or Screen Print?
  3. Check for Webstore: If webstore order, waive automatically.
  4. Review Order History: Look for recent large orders or repeat customer status.
  5. Apply Fee or Waiver: Based on the eligibility matrix above.
  6. Document in Shopworks: Include waiver reason if applicable.
  7. Communicate to Customer: Use the scripts provided below.
  8. Finalize Quote: Ensure system reflects the correct pricing.

Customer Communication Scripts

When Applying the Fee:
"Since your order is under our 24-piece minimum, there's a $50 administrative fee that covers our order processing costs. This is divided across your items, adding $[X] per piece. Would you like to add more items to reach 24 pieces and avoid this fee?"
When Waiving for Fill-In:
"Great news! Since you had that large order with us recently, we'll waive the minimum fee for these additional items as a courtesy."
When Partially Waiving for Loyalty:
"As a valued repeat customer, we're reducing the minimum fee from $50 to $25 for your order. We appreciate your continued business!"
For Screen Print/DTF (No Waiver):
"For screen print and DTF orders under 24 pieces, we apply a $50 minimum fee to cover our administrative costs. This helps us maintain quality service even on smaller orders. The fee is divided across your items."

Frequently Asked Questions

Why do we have this fee?
The LTM fee covers the same administrative costs for a 5-piece order as a 24-piece order - CSR time, purchasing, receiving, and processing. Without this fee, we would lose money on small orders.
Can customers combine orders to avoid the fee?
Yes! We encourage customers to combine orders or add items to reach 24 pieces. This is the best way to avoid the fee and get better per-item pricing.
What if a customer orders exactly 23 pieces?
The fee still applies to orders of 23 pieces. We strongly recommend adding one more item to reach 24 and avoid the $50 fee entirely - it's almost always more cost-effective for the customer.
Does the fee apply to sample orders?
Yes, the LTM fee applies to all orders under 24 pieces, including samples. However, if the sample leads to a large order within 30 days, we may waive the fee retroactively.
What about add-on orders for existing customers?
If the customer had a $500+ order within the last 30 days and needs 1-5 additional items (embroidery or DTG only), we can waive the fee. This doesn't apply to screen print or DTF.
How do I request an exception?
For situations not covered by the standard waiver scenarios, contact Erik directly with the order details and reason for the exception request. Document the approval in Shopworks.
