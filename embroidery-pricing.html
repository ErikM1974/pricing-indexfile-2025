Our state-of-the-art embroidery machines produce high-quality, durable designs that withstand repeated washing and wear. We use premium threads and professional digitizing to ensure your logo or design looks its best.

Embroidery FAQs

What is the maximum size for embroidery?

Standard embroidery areas are typically up to 4" x 4" for most garments. Larger designs may require special placement or additional charges.

How many colors can I include in my embroidery design?

Our standard pricing includes up to 8 thread colors. Additional colors may incur a small upcharge.

What file formats do you accept for embroidery?

We can work with most vector formats (.ai, .eps, .pdf) or high-resolution raster images (.png, .jpg). For best results, vector files are preferred.
